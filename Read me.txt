# PHAGA: Pharma-Grade Alcohol Best Parameters Based on Molasses Parameters by Elhussien

## 🌟 About
This web app helps operators determine the best operational parameters for beet molasses fermentation to produce pharma-grade alcohol.  
It guides on water addition, nitrogen source, pH adjustment, yeast pitch, and flags risks of IPA/methanol formation.
